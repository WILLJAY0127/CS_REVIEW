## 摘要

**Conversation Overview**

The person is learning English using EnglishPod as their primary resource and asked Claude to design a systematic, reusable study method applicable to every episode that covers all four language skills: listening, speaking, reading, and writing. The goal was to establish a repeatable SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) rather than a one-off plan.

Claude developed a five-step framework for each EnglishPod episode totaling approximately 45 minutes: blind listening without a transcript to train real-speed comprehension; intensive listening with the transcript to identify gaps and note connected speech patterns; deep phrase internalization using a structured approach (meaning, context, original sentence construction); shadow reading to bridge passive knowledge and active speaking; and written output at three difficulty levels (sentence creation, story retelling, or writing a new dialogue). The conversation also established a spaced repetition review system: same-day completion of the full SOP, next-day sentence reconstruction from memory without notes, and a one-week re-listening test.

The person's preferred working style, established in prior interactions, carried into this session: Claude explained phrase internalization using a programming analogy (treating phrases like method calls with known inputs, outputs, and use cases). The writing practice step was explicitly tied to their existing correction workflow with Claude—write freely, receive annotated corrections with explanations, then rewrite corrected sentences from memory. Claude recommended beginning immediately with Step 3 using the current episode's four key phrases as a concrete first exercise.

## EnglishPod 每集学习 SOP

**核心理念**：每集约 45 分钟，5 个步骤，听说读写全覆盖，形成一套可以无限复用的流程。

---

### Step 1 · 🎧 盲听（5 分钟）— 训练真实语速的耳朵

**不看文字稿**，完整听一遍。听完后立刻写下：
- 大意是什么？（餐厅场景？什么问题？）
- 听清楚了哪些词？
- 哪些地方听不清？（打个时间戳，比如 "01:23 附近"）

> 💡 很多人跳过这步，直接看文字稿——这会让耳朵永远依赖眼睛。盲听才是真正的听力训练。

---

### Step 2 · 📄 精听 + 文字稿（10 分钟）— 填补漏洞

对照文字稿再听一遍：
- 标出**所有不认识的词和短语**
- 重点听主持人重点讲解的那 4–6 个表达（这集是：*still working on it / grab / complimentary / I'll go with*）
- 注意**连读和弱化音**，比如 "I'll go with" 在自然语速里听起来像 "aigo wih"

比较 Step 1 和 Step 2 的差距——这个差距缩小的过程就是你进步的轨迹。

---

### Step 3 · 🧠 短语内化（10 分钟）— 深度理解，不只是"认识"

整理本集核心短语，每个短语做三件事：

| 短语 | 意思 | 场景 | 你的造句 |
|---|---|---|---|
| I'm still working on it | 还没做完，需要更多时间 | 被催进度时 | （你来写）|
| grab | 快速去拿/买 | 随手做某事 | （你来写）|
| complimentary | 免费赠送的 | 服务业场景 | （你来写）|
| I'll go with... | 我选... | 做决定时 | （你来写）|

把你的造句发给我 → 我用咱们之前的 correction 格式帮你批改 → 你默写改正后的版本。

> 💡 类比 Java：短语就像方法调用，你要知道它的参数（用什么场景）、返回值（达到什么效果），不只是知道方法名。

---

### Step 4 · 🗣️ 影子跟读（10 分钟）— 让嘴巴追上大脑

用文字稿跟着音频逐句模仿，重点模仿**节奏、语调、停顿**，不只是发音：

1. **逐句跟读** → 暂停，跟一句
2. **整段跟读** → 不暂停，跟上去
3. **角色扮演** → 今天扮演服务员 Fabio，明天扮演那个难搞的客人

可选：录音 → 回听 → 找自己和原版的差距。

> 💡 这步是让"脑子里认识"变成"嘴巴会说"的关键桥梁。

---

### Step 5 · ✍️ 写作输出（10 分钟）— 用了才算真的会

三个难度选一个，视当天时间和状态决定：

- **基础**：用今天 4 个核心短语各造一个句子
- **进阶**：用自己的话把今天的故事重述出来（3–5 句话）
- **挑战**：写一个类似场景的新对话（比如：你去咖啡店，咖啡师向你推荐今日特供）

写完发给我批改，然后默写改正版。
